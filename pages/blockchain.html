Blockchain

Computer network will keep track of information

Blockchain technology is a decentralized record of all transactions across the P2P digital network. Users can confirm and reference transactions without the need for a central authority to certify.

At this stage, your transaction's new block of information is added to the existing blockchain. Once the new information is added, it becomes permanent and unalterable. At this point, it is set in stone; or in this case set in a digital block of information.
